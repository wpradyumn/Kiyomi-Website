About Kiyomi

Welcome to Kiyomi Café, where serenity vibes meet culinary excellence in the heart of Surat. Our comforting theme creates a refreshing escape from the city hustle, offering a perfect blend of relaxation and indulgence.

At Kiyomi, we pride ourselves on our multicuisine magic, bringing together flavors from around the world in one vibrant space. Whether you're looking for a cozy breakfast spot, a relaxed lunch venue, or an enchanting dinner destination, our diverse menu and warm atmosphere make every visit special.

Founded with a passion for creating memorable dining experiences, Kiyomi Café has quickly become a favorite gathering place for food enthusiasts, friends, and families alike.
